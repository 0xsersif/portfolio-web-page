About Me

My journey in technology has been non-traditional but focused on continuous learning:

After completing my high school degree in Mathematics and Engineering, I began my tech journey at a Higher School of Applied Science studying Networks and Communications. Though I didn't complete the formal program, this experience sparked my passion for technology.

I redirected my education through self-guided learning, beginning with Harvard's CS50 course which provided me with strong computer science fundamentals. This foundation allowed me to systematically build my skills in front-end development, mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Currently, I'm expanding my expertise through the Moroccan Digital Academy, specializing in blockchain technology and generative AI. This combination of self-directed learning and specialized training has given me a unique perspective and practical skill set focused on emerging technologies.
